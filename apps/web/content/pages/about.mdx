# I'm Nomu.

I build software, run experiments, and explore ideas at the intersection of systems, design, and human experience. I document the process as I go.

## What I do

I build tools and systems that solve real problems and help me think more clearly. Lately, that includes developer tools, writing apps, and focused utilities.

## Interests

Systems thinking, product design, developer experience, artificial intelligence, and the craft of writing. I enjoy things that are useful, elegant, and a little bit weird.

## Approach

I value clarity over cleverness, steady progress over perfection, and constraints that make the work more legible.
