Reading Time Calculator estimates duration from a pasted draft and a configurable reading speed. It also reports word and character counts.

## An estimate, not a promise

Code, unfamiliar terms, diagrams, and dense arguments slow readers differently. Use the number as a planning aid and pair it with good structure.

<Aside title="Default">The initial estimate uses 238 words per minute and can be adjusted.</Aside>
