Palette Ratio Checker makes the proportion of colors explicit. Add palette values, assign each a share, and compare the resulting balance as a strip and percentage table.

## Balance before decoration

Useful palettes are not only collections of compatible swatches. Their proportions determine whether an interface feels quiet, dense, or fragmented.

<Callout title="Starting point">Try 80% foundation, 15% secondary, and 5% emphasis, then adjust for the actual content.</Callout>
